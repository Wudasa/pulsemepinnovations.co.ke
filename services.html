Electrical Services

PulseMEP offers a comprehensive range of electrical building services designed to meet the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and innovation.

Our expertise spans from power distribution systems and energy-efficient lighting to backup power solutions, ensuring uninterrupted operations for our clients. We are also adept in renewable energy systems, integrating solar power and other sustainable technologies into building designs.

Our services include advanced fire detection and alarm systems, security and access control, as well as data and communication networks. We prioritize smart building solutions, incorporating automation for energy management and centralized control. With a strong focus on safety, we provide earthing, lightning protection, and electrical safety audits to ensure all installations are compliant with BS7677 and future-proof.

Our Electrical Capabilities:

  • Power distribution systems
  • Energy-efficient lighting design
  • Backup power & renewable energy integration
  • Fire detection & alarm systems
  • Security and access control
  • Electrical safety audits & compliance

Plumbing & Drainage Services

PulseMEP provides expert plumbing and drainage services that are integral to the functionality and sustainability of any building.

Our team specializes in the design and installation of efficient water supply systems, ensuring a reliable flow of clean water throughout the project. We develop customized drainage solutions, including wastewater treatment systems and sustainable rainwater harvesting designs to minimize environmental impact.

Our expertise extends to hot water generation systems, greywater recycling, and sanitary plumbing for commercial, residential, industrial, healthcare, and hospitality developments.

Our Plumbing Capabilities:

  • Water supply & distribution systems
  • Drainage and sewerage design
  • Hot water systems (solar & boilers)
  • Rainwater harvesting & greywater recycling
  • Sanitary plumbing for multiple sectors
